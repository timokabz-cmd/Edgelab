§ MAIN OBJECTS

What we're registered — and built — to do.

Our Memorandum sets out three main objects. Each one is a working practice inside the lab, not a line on paper.

01
Systems

Advanced computing, AI & systems integration

We research, build and integrate AI platforms, machine learning architectures and automated workflows — engineering SaaS infrastructure and custom digital applications that raise operational and financial performance for businesses and institutions.

02
Capacity

Technological capacity building & instruction

We design and run training programs and workshops in prompt engineering, interface design and digital literacy — closing the execution gap between emerging tools and the people who need to use them.

03
Media

Multimedia production, advertising & digital assets

We produce digital content, audiovisual work and intellectual property, and put it to work through sponsorships, brand partnerships and affiliate channels across current and emerging media.
